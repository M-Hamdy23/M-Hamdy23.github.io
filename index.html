    Nutrien Interactive HGV Driver Training Courses

    Snippet from one of Nutrien’s interactive HGV driver training courses


    The Problem

    Nutrien’s experienced heavy goods and hazardous materials truck drivers were facing uncommon accidents which don’t have any specific existing training due to their rarity. They needed an all-in-one training solution that allowed drivers to practice and master the correct actions to take in these scenarios.


    THE SOLUTION – 7 Interactive HGV Training courses

    We developed 7 bespoke scenarios to address the specific accidents identified by Nutrien. Each scenario offers an instructional training phase, a practice phase without hints, and an assessment mode that seamlessly integrates with Nutrien’s existing LMS. Users would access the different modules via 2 modes, Train and Practice mode, and a separate final assessment mode.
